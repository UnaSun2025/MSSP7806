Methodology

Core Formulas

  • Revenue Base = Investment * capTurnover * 0.45 + Employees * revenuePerEmployee * 0.35 + Capacity * revenuePerCapacityUnit * 0.20
  • Operating Profit = Revenue * margin
  • Tax Burden = Corporate Tax + Payroll Tax + Property Tax (proxy)
  • Net Profit = Operating Profit - Tax Burden - Energy Cost
  • Labor Cost Share = Annual Payroll / Revenue

Scenario Parameters (Base)

  • Revenue growth: 4.0% annually
  • Payroll growth: 3.0% annually
  • Tax and energy escalation: 2.5% annually
  • Electricity rate basis: PA state benchmark by use class ([C7])

Included in Model

  • Five-year directional revenue/profit/tax/energy scenario outputs
  • County context overlays and indicative policy screening
  • Rule-based permitting wizard and checklist outputs

Excluded from Model

  • Financing structure and debt service schedules
  • Depreciation schedules, bonus depreciation, and firm-specific tax elections
  • Detailed grant award probabilities or binding incentive determinations
  • Municipality-specific fee schedules and legal adjudication outcomes

Disclaimers

This tool provides planning estimates only. Outputs are directional and are not legal, tax, accounting, engineering, underwriting, or investment advice.

All policy and permitting content should be treated as typical/varies screening guidance. Final eligibility and approval outcomes depend on formal submissions, program cycles, site specifics, and agency determinations.

Typical requirements vary by municipality; verify with local officials.
